Starting out is difficult because it is hard to be the 'new guy'. It is hard to face the fact there's people who are so much better than you that they're out of your reach. But it is also extremely important to work through this feeling and to persist through the embarassment of being the newbie, because one day, shoveling all that fertilizer and moistening that soil is going to pay off.
