# Savings
## Pay yourself first
Allocate funds for savings first on any income
## Needs first
In any budget, cover the essentials before anything else
## 50/30/20 rule
50% Needs
30% Wants
20% Savings or Debt
**BE CLEAR WITH WANTS AND NEEDS**

# Credit
Take only as much credit as you can pay off in a month (be very selective)

# Emergency Fund
Keep and emergency fund worth 3-6 months of income as security against losses.
**KEEP IT LIQUID AND EASY TO ACCESS**
