> PPL x ARNOLD

Forearm exercsies everyday
Legs+shoulders and Legs+Core
Strength and Hypertrophy focused days

## Day 1 – Push (Chest + Triceps + Forearms)

- Flat barbell bench press — 4×4–6
- Flat DB bench press — 3×6–10
- Machine or cable chest press — 3×8–12
- Cable or pec-deck fly — 3×10–15
- Close-grip bench press or weighted dip — 3×6–8
- Rope triceps pressdown — 3×10–15
- Overhead DB or cable triceps extension — 2–3×10–12
- Forearms: farmer’s carry or wrist curls — 2×20–30 s or 2×12–20

## Day 2 – Pull (Back + Biceps + Forearms)

- Weighted pull-up or lat pulldown — 4×4–6
- Barbell or chest-supported row — 4×4–6
- One-arm DB row — 3×8–12
- Straight-arm pulldown — 3×10–15
- Barbell or EZ-bar curl — 3×6–10
- Incline DB curl or hammer curl — 3×8–12
- Face pull or rear-delt row — 3×12–15
- Forearms: dead hang or hammer curl hold — 2×20–30 s

## Day 3 – Legs + Shoulders + Forearms (Mixed Strength/Hypertrophy)

_Legs_
- Back or front squat — 4×4–6
- Romanian deadlift — 3×6–8
- Leg press or hack squat — 3×8–12
- Leg curl — 3×10–15
- Seated or standing calf raise — 3×8–12

_Shoulders_
- Seated barbell or DB overhead press — 3–4×6–8
- Lateral raise — 3×10–15
- Rear-delt raise or face pull — 3×10–15

_Forearms_
- Reverse curls — 2×10–15

## Day 4 – Chest (Incline) + Back + Forearms

_Chest (Incline-focused)_
- Incline barbell bench press — 4×6–8
- Incline DB press — 3×8–12
- Incline or high-to-low cable fly — 3×10–15

_Back_
- Pull-up or lat pulldown (moderate load) — 3×8–10
- Barbell or machine row — 3×8–12
- Pullover or straight-arm pulldown — 3×10–15

_Forearms_
- Barbell static hold or wrist roller — 2–3×20–30 s or trips

## Day 5 – Shoulders + Arms + Forearms

_Shoulders_
- Arnold press or machine shoulder press — 3×8–12
- Lateral raise (DB or cable) — 3×12–15
- Rear-delt fly or cable reverse fly — 3×12–15

_Arms_
- Barbell or EZ-bar curl — 3×8–12
- Incline DB curl or hammer curl — 3×10–12
- Skullcrusher or overhead triceps extension — 3×8–12
- Rope pressdown — 3×10–15

_Forearms_
- Plate pinches or gripper — 2×max hold

## Day 6 – Legs (Strength-biased) + Core + Forearms

_Legs_
- Deadlift or safety-bar/front squat — 5×3 (heavy)
- Bulgarian split squat — 3×6–8
- Leg press or belt squat — 3×8–10
- Leg curl — 2–3×8–12
- Standing calf raise — 3×6–10

_Core_
- Hanging leg raise — 3×10–15
- Cable woodchop — 3×12–15/side
- Plank or ab-wheel — 3×20–40 s

_Forearms_
- Wrist curls or reverse wrist curls — 2×12–20
