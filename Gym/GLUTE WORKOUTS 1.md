# Exercises
## Deficit Reverse Lunge
- Don't rely on the support hand
- No weight on back leg (back leg is just support)
- Sit back into the glute

## RDL and SLDL
Knee bend on RDL
No bend in SLDL

## Hip Thrust
- Stiff core
- Straight back
- Knees at 90 at the top

## Bulgarians
- To start - Put rear foot on bench, sit down, leg out and then stand up

# Maximize Gains

- Time under tension (slow concentric, explosive eccentric)
