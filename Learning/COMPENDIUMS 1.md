# What is a Compendium?

A Compendium is an organised collection of knowledge.
- Systematically Presented
- Comprehensive in scope (fully fleshed out)

# How to study using Compendiums

After studying, you need to sit down and write everything you learned into your compendium.
Write down concepts learned as well as goals (in separate sections).

**This is not a notebook.** Research sufficiently before recording in the compendium. Remember - **comprehensive in scope.**
